Update GIT token:
1. Go to:
https://github.com/settings/tokens/1276390802/regenerate
2. Hit "Update token"

To check the current token in RStudio
1. Open RStudio
2. Type
install.packages("gitcreds")
library(gitcreds)
gitcreds_set()
3. Choose option 3
See the password/token
